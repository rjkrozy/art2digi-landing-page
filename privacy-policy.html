Who we are

This website is operated by Art2Digi Solutions, founded and owned by Rutsella Krosendijk, based in Aruba. We offer natural light branding photography, workshops and training services.

You can reach us at any time via the contact form on this website or through WhatsApp at +297 563 8194.

What data we collect

We only collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us through our forms:

  • Contact form — your name, email address and message
  • Workshop registration form — your name, email address and phone number

We do not collect any data automatically, we do not use cookies and we do not use any tracking or analytics tools on this website.

How we use your data

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your message or enquiry
  • Process and confirm your workshop registration
  • Send you payment instructions after registration
  • Follow up on your enquiry or project

We will never sell, share or pass your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

How we store your data

Form submissions are processed through Netlify Forms, a secure service provided by Netlify Inc. Your data is stored on Netlify's servers. You can read Netlify's privacy policy at netlify.com/privacy.

We keep your data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected. After that, we delete it.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request that we correct or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the contact form or WhatsApp at +297 563 8194. We will respond within 30 days.

Photography and portfolio

Photos of clients published on this website are shared with the explicit permission of the individuals pictured. If you are a client and wish to have your photos removed from this website, please contact us and we will act immediately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Questions?
