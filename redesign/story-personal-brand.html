"Every brand has a face, a feeling, a moment of confidence ready to be captured. I worked with Annaica to find hers."

Annaica needed new photos. LinkedIn, Teams, social media. The usual ask. But I don't do usual.

I came with an idea. Two locations. Two sides of the same person. I go with the vibe of my client. I ask what they want me to capture. Then I come with the crazy ideas. She was up for all of it.

Location 1 — Malmok

Playful. Free. Completely herself.

Natural light, rocks, water, trees. No studio. No backdrop. Just Annaica in her element laughing, moving and forgetting the camera was there.

Confident. Warm. Still her.
