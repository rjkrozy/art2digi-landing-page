The Sunset Chasing Girl

I fell in love with photography the moment I captured my first sunset. That golden light that takes your breath away. I've been chasing it ever since.

I shoot in natural light because it gives you the real energy of a moment — nothing added, nothing faked. I work with what's there and find the story in it.

I'm wired to go all in. Half measures don't exist in my world.

I will capture your story exactly as it deserves to be.

Rutsella Krosendijk
